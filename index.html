About Us

INF Robotics has created RUDY, the premier in-home care companion, which helps older adults stay active, engaged and connected to their care community. RUDY is a pro-active companion that provides a level of services only matched by a human in the home.

Your life is mobile, so should your solution be.

AI Technology

INF Robotics patented mobile AI technology, award winning platform and intuitive interaction, creates a quick and trusting bond between RUDY and the older adult, allowing for more effective delivery of RUDY’s capabilities.

Established

INF Robotics was founded in the U.S. under the core values of easy to use, affordable and safe technology to keep older adults in their home. Our team has all experienced the pain of a loved one having to leave their home due to lack of solutions on the market to address this problem. We are committed to helping older adults maintain their independence with RUDY.

Technology

INF Robotics creates technology in Mobile Cloud Robotics, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Cognitive Computing, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning to enable an emotional bond between people and our devices. This maximizes the use time while increasing efficiency.

Our technology enables devices to make favorable decisions for the user’s well-being, learn and adapt around the user’s needs as they change over time. RUDY is your friend, staying a step ahead to keep you independent.

Rudy

RUDY is the premier intuitive mobile social robot, that is designed to promote an active and independent lifestyle, while keeping you connected to your care community. We have designed RUDY alongside older adults, their family and caregivers.

1

Activity engagement that is customized for each individual
-Stay active physically and keep your mind sharp

2

Easy to use and intuitive
-Simple straightforward voice control specifically for older adults, uses your movements and environment, offers encouraging and positive personality

3

Caregivers and family can access services
-Stay connected to caregivers, family or help if you need it in any room, on-demand

4

Stay mobile and independent
-Your lifestyle is not based in one room, keep RUDY with you to help throughout your day about your home.

5

Keep track of your schedule and reminders
-Stay on schedule and independent with personalized schedule management tools to fit your needs.
